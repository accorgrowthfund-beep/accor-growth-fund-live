Our Investment Strategies

Accor Growth Fund pursues a diversified, sector-agnostic strategy focused on scalable businesses, strong fundamentals, and selective IPO opportunities with disciplined value-conscious investing.

We focus on identifying companies with strong growth potential, backed by sound management teams and solid fundamentals. Our evaluation emphasizes robust balance sheets, healthy cash flows, and consistent earnings growth to ensure long-term value creation.

Our team actively monitors portfolio companies and remains closely aligned with real-time market developments. This proactive approach enables us to make timely investment decisions and respond effectively to evolving business and market dynamics.

Risk management is central to our portfolio construction, with diversification playing a key role in minimizing concentration risk. By spreading exposure across industries and market caps, we aim to cushion volatility while preserving strong growth potential.

While we actively seek growth opportunities, our approach remains firmly rooted in valuation discipline. We invest only when a stock is reasonably priced relative to its future earnings potential and overall business outlook.

We selectively participate in IPO and Pre-IPO opportunities to capture high-growth potential in innovative companies. Each investment is assessed with the same rigorous fundamental research and valuation discipline that we apply to listed securities, ensuring consistency and quality in our approach.
